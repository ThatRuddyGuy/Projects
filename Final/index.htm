The N7 Soldiers

N7 is a special section of the Systems Alliance Military. The "N" declares special forces, and the "7" is indicative of the highest level of training the soldier has gone through. Earning the N7 badge is reserved for the wisest and greatest soldiers out there. Often, N7 soldiers are quickly placed in leadership roles to better benefit the military as a whole.

The First Step
