Our Mission

Those individuals that are strong enough and lucky enough to make it through our training program move on to become members of our elite special forces. These fortunate few are tasked with the privilege of venturing out, locating, and engaging the threats against our universe. The N7 forces are constantly on the move, taking down any and all dangers they come across. Because of these heroes, your daily lives continue on uninterrupted and peacefully.
